Who We Are

We are members of the Swarthmore Community organizing for improved labor conditions, such as higher wages, improved working conditions, and better employee benefits, at Swarthmore College. As a hub for labor organizing activity at Swarthmore College, Solidarity at Swat will advocate and organize for student, staff members, and faculty who want want to see improved labor conditions. We believe it is unacceptable for Swarthmore College to pay less than a living wage to any of its workers or deny benefits and services, such as health insurance or child care. If the administration chooses to hide behind technicalities and bureaucracy, we aim to force their hand. There is room for all who labor in Solidarity at Swat. Join us and fight for a world that realizes a guiding truth: "all labor has dignity" - Martin Luther King Jr.
